About ElectroSource

Our mission is to provide professional-grade electrical components at competitive prices, backed by expert customer support. With over a decade of experience, we've built a reputation for excellence.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the shipping timeline?

Orders placed before 2 PM are processed same-day. Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. We also offer expedited shipping for rush orders.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes! Orders over 50 units receive a 15% discount. For larger quantities, contact our sales team for custom quotes and volume pricing.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days if products are unused and in original packaging. Full refunds are provided, with free return shipping on orders over $50.

Are your products internationally certified?

All our products meet UL, CSA, and European CE standards. Certificates are available upon request for compliance verification.

How can I get technical support?

Contact our technical team at support@electrosource.com or call 1-800-CABLES. Average response time is under 1 hour during business hours.

Can I mix and match products for bulk orders?

Mix and match any products in your order. Bulk discounts apply to total quantity across all product types.
